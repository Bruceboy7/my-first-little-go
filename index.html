Why Must Coders Be Bold

Learning to code is a journey. The road is filled with pitfalls, ominous mountains of information calling out to be understood, joyful release when a program comes together, etc. The more the coder pours themselves into their work, the more change it will affect them on a more varying scale; higher highs and lower lows. That makes sense, right? If someone barely cares about the coding their doing, they're not going to feel the heaviness of an error message after a long day of coding. Likewise, they wont feel the ecstasy of a perfectly running program after grueling edits. Coding, and learning its language, is not for everyone, but can be truly rewarding.
